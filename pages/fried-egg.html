Amazing Fried Egg

Ingredients

  • 1-2 egg(s)
  • 1 teaspoon butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  • Step 1

    Heat your favorite skillet over medium-high heat. Once the skillet is nice and hot, break the egg.

  • Step 2

    If you prefer the yolk to be runny, cook the egg for about 3-4 minutes If you want a cooked-yolk, put the cover on the skillet so it will help cook the yolk with the hot air trapped inside

  • Step 3

    Move the egg from skillet to pan, put salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
